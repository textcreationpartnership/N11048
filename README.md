The firſt queſtion that ariſes is, Whether Mercury is active in its crude ſtate, as is not (in that) acted on by the vegetable acid? This, however, is ſomewhat doubted; but ſome ſpeak much of its efficacy, and Dover gave it in a variety of diſeaſes; and when this is the caſe, a medicine will frequently reap that honour which is only due to nature.
